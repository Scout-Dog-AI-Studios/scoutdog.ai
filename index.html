About the Studio

Scout Dog AI Studios LLC is a boutique software development firm based in Windsor, California. We specialize in building lean, high-utility applications that leverage modern AI to solve real-world problems in the physical market.

Our mission is to create tools that help people identify, value, and preserve the items they care about. We believe that technology is at its best when it serves as a bridge between digital intelligence and the physical world.

Our Focus

  • Intelligent Valuation: Utilizing advanced vision models and grounding technology to provide instant, accurate market data for physical goods.
  • Specialized Marketplaces: Building community-centric platforms that facilitate the exchange of vintage gear and specialized equipment.
  • Lean Engineering: Operating as a solo-led studio allows us to prioritize depth, privacy, and precision in every line of code we ship.
